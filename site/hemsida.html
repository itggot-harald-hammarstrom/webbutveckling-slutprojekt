Welcome to Snuswiki! This is the best site for the everyday snuser. If you don't already know snus is a swedish tobbaco product that is better and healthier than most other uses of tobacco. It is consumed by either placing it under your lip or inhaling it through your nose (this version is called snuff). Snus comes in two different types which are lös (loose) or portion which is packed in a small bag. It also comes in a multitude of different tastes and nikotine amounts.
